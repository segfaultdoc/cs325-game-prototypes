Multi Tasker

Use the right and left arrows. To use the pumpkin then hold down the '3' key at the top of the keyboard along with the arrows, hold the 2 and while use the arrows for the wooden bucket, hold the 1 and the arrows for the other bucket. Also the if no numbers are held while using the arrows, then it defaults to the pumpkin. Use the space bar to stop all buckets from moving. Clicking the mouse pointer in the game screen resets the game.
